Example of an EPA rule potentially affecting a very large number of NAICS and facilities:
Methylene Chloride; Regulation Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)
Proposed Rule
5/3/2023
https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2023/05/03/2023-09184/methylene-chloride-regulation-under-the-toxic-substances-control-act-tsca

You may be potentially affected by the proposed action if you manufacture (defined under TSCA to include import), process, distribute in commerce, use, or dispose of methylene chloride or products containing methylene chloride.
